Welcome: Test your intuition and logic in this exciting challenge where each level pushes your guessing skills to the limit.
Level: Each level tracks your ability to guess the correct number. With every level increase, the length of the randomly generated number doubles, making the game progressively more challenging.
Points: The scoring uses a two decimal currency style format for precision. Correct full guess Earn 5 multiplied by the current level points. Correct digit in correct position Gain 0.10 points per unique digit. Correct digit in wrong position Receive 0.05 points per unique digit.
Points Calculated: 0.01 pts one digit exists. 0.05 pts half of the digits exists. 0.10 pts one digit is in the correct position. 0.25 pts half of the digits in the correct position. 1.00 pts when the guess is correct.
Randomly generated number display: The target number is hash encrypted and gradually revealed as you guess digits correctly.
User guess display: Shows your current guess in real time, regardless of length.
Button display: Includes digits 0 though 9, the C (Clear) Resets your guess and starts a new history segment. ‚Üê (Backspace) Removes the last digit and logs the change in history.
History display: Tracks every guess change with timestamps. Revisit previous guesses. without affecting points or failed attempts. Delete any history segment at will.
Challenge levels: Triggered randomly within a level. The challenge number length equals your total failed attempts in that level. Challenge number size resets when you advance to the next level.
